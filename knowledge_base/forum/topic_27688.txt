QUESTION (by anmehta @ 2023-07-28T13:34:37.019Z):
Dear experts,
I am trying to estimate a background process using the alpha ratio method. In nutshell, the background shape is obtained in the signal region by multiplying the background shape in the side-band region obtained after fit to data times the transfer factor (alpha) where this alpha is simply a MC-based ratio of SR to SB region. To add uncertainty on this background estimation, I want to vary the shape of background in the SB region using another functional form and similarly for alpha.
Could you please suggest a way out to implement this using workspaces?
Best,
Ankita

REPLY 2 (by anigamov @ 2023-07-31T09:00:07.582Z):
Dear Ankita,
I think the most optimal way would be to use the discrete profiling method [0] and define the various pdfs as RooMultiPdf. There are instructions available on how to implement it in Combine documentation pages [1] as well as from the most recent Combine tutorial [2-3].  I hope this helps to get started, let me know if something is unclear.
Best,
Aliya
