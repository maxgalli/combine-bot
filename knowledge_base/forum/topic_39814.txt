REPLY 2 (by vischia @ 2024-04-25T06:40:25.000Z):
Dear Antonio,
you have in your data card a line like “autoMCStats…”, right?
That line defines for you a nuisance parameter per channel per bin, corresponding to joining together the uncertainties due to the finite amount of Monte Carlo events of the various processes. You used to have to create one template per bin per channel by yourself and them sum them up according to the Barlow–Beeston procedure, and put all these templates and list all these nuisance parameters in the data cards, but nowadays combine does that for you.
It’s documented in
https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/latest/part2/bin-wise-stats/
Cheers,
Pietro
