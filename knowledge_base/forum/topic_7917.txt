REPLY 2 (by bregnery @ 2022-03-11T09:21:27.076Z):
Hi Prafulla,
The idea of the B2G goodness of fit test is to run the goodness of fit test on a validation (or measurement) region. This is a separate region from the signal region where one can measure nuisance parameters and test the health of the fit before fitting the signal region.
A description of how to generate toys while blinded and using a measurement region is located here: https://cms-b2g.docs.cern.ch/combine/GenerateToys/
The outline of the B2G GoF test is here: https://cms-b2g.docs.cern.ch/combine/StatTests/
The main point is don’t unblind the signal region, but check the health of the fit in orthogonal regions.
I hope this helps.

Brendan
