QUESTION (by jipark @ 2026-03-02T15:36:31.058Z):
Dear Combine experts,
During the Combine review [1] of TOP-25-011 [2], we observed some unexpected behavior in the impact calculation. In short, we found small differences in the impact values and their ranking before and after renaming some of the nuisance parameters.
Although these differences are negligible for the expected uncertainties on POIs (r, CMS_TOP25011_mass, CMS_TOP25011_width), the ordering of impacts changes slightly in a few places. We cross-checked this using the latest version of Combine (v10.5) and confirmed that the effect persists. The contents of the datacard and the input shapes have been double-checked and found to be consistent.
Our current understanding is that the differences may be related to the internal ordering of the nuisance parameters, possibly affected by their names. To investigate this further, we partially restored some parameter names from the original datacard and also manually modified several parameter names on top of the datacard provided in the repository [1]. By doing so, we were able to alter some of the impact rankings, as shown in the attached slides.
We are not sure if this is a known or expected behavior. We would greatly appreciate any advice on further tests or checks we could perform.
Best,
Jiwon
