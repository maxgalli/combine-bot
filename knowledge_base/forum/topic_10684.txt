QUESTION (by lguzzi @ 2022-05-20T09:59:50.631Z):
Dear experts,
I am working on the tau->3mu analysis, which looks for a bump in the three-muon invariant mass distribution at the tau mass value. The analysis aims to set an upper limit on the tau->3mu decay rate.
To do so, we use combine and the HybridNew method.
I am implementing discrete profiling [1] into the analysis workflow to allow for multiple choices of the background function. Having defined a list of pdfs which goes into the RooMultiPdf (which is the object I feed to combine to use as the background pdf), which one will be used by combine to generate toys?
thank you,
Luca
[1] http://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part3/nonstandard/#discrete-profiling
