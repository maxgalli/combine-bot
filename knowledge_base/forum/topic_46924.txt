REPLY 2 (by amarini @ 2024-08-28T16:53:47.423Z):
Hi,
I think you are correct, the offending characters are the dot(s) in any number. Only characters matching letters digits and underscore (i.e., regex \w) currently match the variables names correctly.
this seems to be related to the processing of RooFormulaVar in newer (?) versions of ROOT/RooFit, though it seems to be also pretty established (>6.18 ?TBC).
I believe that this is triggered by this line  and the matches in the RooFormulaVar constructions are in RooFit at the line 249 here.
I managed to have a working setup w/ RooFit 6.12 in CMSSW_10_2_13. We may try to find a patch, but I don’t know if it comes in the near future.
For the moment, you can change the channel name with combineCards (at least for single channel cards as your) should work out-of-the-box, as below. Maybe use _ instead of dots .)
combineCards.py ABC=HToZATo2L2B_bb_associatedProduction_nb2PLusnb3_resolved_boosted_OSSF_mbb_MH_335.4_MA_82.14.dat > ABC.dat
text2workspace.py -m 125 -o ABC.root ABC.dat
combine -M FitDiagnostics --skipBOnlyFit --skipSBFit --saveShapes -v3  -n test_ABC ABC.root

-Andrea
