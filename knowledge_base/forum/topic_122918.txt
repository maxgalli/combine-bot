REPLY 2 (by gortona @ 2025-04-10T08:25:58.000Z):
Hi Sasha,
I think the statistical committee would be better placed to answer, but I believe that’s correct, yes. That’s basically like taking the weighted average of independent measurements.
Although even if the regions are mutually exclusive, I would expect some parameters like luminosity or detector effects to be correlated, simply because all data are taken with the same experiment, so I don’t think this actually applies to CMS measurements except for very fringe cases.
Cheers,
Giacomo
