QUESTION (by gmelachr @ 2026-01-28T16:02:19.635Z):
Dear experts,
for the BPH-24-005 analysis, I am trying to do a simultaneous fit using COMBINE. The purpose is to use the formula N(rare) = dB/dq2 x N(J/ψ) x (αxε(rare) / αxε(J/ψ)) x  Deltaq / BR(J/ψ→μμ) in order to measure the factor dB/dq2. To do this, I define N(rare) and N(J/ψ) (which are the normalizations of the signal processes in the two datasets) as rateParam in the datacard. Moreover, in the datacard, the expected yields for all the processes are set to 1 since the yields are calculated directly from the data. The Deltaq and BR factors are treated as constants. The αxε factors are measured from the MC. Since the yields are calculated from the data, and there are not expected yields in the datacard, the "r” parameter is frozen and in order to take into account the systematic uncertainties, I have replaced the αxε factor with a RooFormulaVar of the form αxε → αxε(1+sum(nuisance * delta(a*e)). I have built the workspace, the fit is converging and I am trying to break down the uncertainty to statistical and systematic, but the values of the POI are different for the stat+syst and stat only fits.
The commands that I used are:
combine -M MultiDimFit mumuLambda_11_rare_Jpsi.txt --freezeParameters r -n .bestfit.with_syst --saveWorkspace --redefineSignalPOIs dBdq2_mumuLambda_q1,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q2,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q3,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q4,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q5,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q6,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q7,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q8,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q9,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q10,dBdq2_mumuLambda_q11
and
combine -M MultiDimFit higgsCombine.bestfit.with_syst.MultiDimFit.mH120.root --freezeParameters r,allConstrainedNuisances -n .scan.with_syst.statonly_correct --snapshotName MultiDimFit --saveWorkspace
I have also attached the datacard mumuLambda_11_rare_Jpsi.txt (10.2 KB) and the stat+syst and stat only workspaces in this txt file workspaces.txt (44.5 KB)
Could you please help me understand why are the values of SignalPOIs shifted between the two fits?
Thank you,
