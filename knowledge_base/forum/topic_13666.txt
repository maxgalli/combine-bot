REPLY 2 (by agilbert @ 2022-08-05T13:57:53.159Z):
Hi Deepak,
So there’s no guarantee that the data/MC ratio for all bins should go closer to 1 in the fit. It is the overall log-likelihood that is minimized, not the contribution from each bin. We can see in your plot that the agreement in the most high stats regions is improved by reducing the overall normalisation of the backgrounds, which in likelihood terms is going to be preferable to shifting the normalisation upwards to improve the agreement in the bins you highlight. So in conclusion I don’t think there’s anything to worry about. (though there’s a potential typo in one of your autoMCStats lines: “0 10 1” → “10 0 1”).
