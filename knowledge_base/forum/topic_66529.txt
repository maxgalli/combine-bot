REPLY 3 (by andrey @ 2024-11-22T15:24:45.331Z):
Hi Aliya,
Thanks a lot for your input.
I now understood that the error above comes from trying to run T2W on a .root file with shapes, which is inside my cards directory.
And I also need to combine the cards with --cc option to the script.
So, the following command seems to work for me:
combineTool.py -M T2W --cc combined.txt -o ws.root -i input/*.txt --parallel 6

Reference:  http://cms-analysis.github.io/CombineHarvester/limits.html
