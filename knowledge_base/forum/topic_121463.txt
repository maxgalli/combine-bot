REPLY 2 (by agilbert @ 2025-04-01T09:26:18.535Z):
Dear Meng,
Some bias to the mean fitted mu in toys is expected when the number of events is small in some bins and not all systematics have a symmetric effect. For autoMCStats I guess some number of these get a per-process Poisson uncertainty? This can make the distribution over toys asymmetric and shift the mean. You could test forcing Gaussian uncertainties in all cases in autoMCStats and see if that changes things. Could also track the median of the mu distribution over toys, hopefully that is closer to the target.
Seeing a larger relative bias with smaller injected signal would be expected if it’s coming from an asymmetry in the background uncertainty.
cheers,
Andrew
