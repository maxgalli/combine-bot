*** Discussion title: Higgs Combination
Dear Combine experts,
We have a binned shape analysis that is finishing the last pre-approval
comments. Amongst them is a request to "Please provide the pre/post-fit
envelopes of the ISR/FSR templates." Currently I am using the
Combine-Harvester PostFitShapesFromWorkspace to produce histograms
(using as input a fit performed on the non-blinded analysis bins).
Isolating a single nuisance does not appear to be an option at that step
(I presume that the fit option --saveWithUncertainties will also only
produce a total uncertainty template), and I have a recommendation to
try nuisance freezing in the fit to accomplish this (
https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part2/settinguptheanalysis.html#manipulation-of-nuisance-parameters
).
In this case, how do I ensure the nuisances sit at the central post-fit
value, but do not contribute to the uncertainty band calculation? If
they're frozen, will I be able to use the Cov. Matrix, or should
--sampling be turned off for PostFitShapesFromWorkspace? Or perhaps only
the nuisance of interest should be frozen, and inferred from the
uncertainties with and without it floating in the fit?
If there are alternative approaches, suggestions are welcome.
