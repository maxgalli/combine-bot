REPLY 2 (by adewit @ 2024-07-16T11:45:33.410Z):
Dear Jieun,
You should make histograms of the mass with the JMR and JMS uncertainties varied (each uncertainty independently) - even if the JMR and JMS only modify the pT directly, this can have an effect on other variables (such as the mass that you reconstruct).
Hope that helps,
Adinda
