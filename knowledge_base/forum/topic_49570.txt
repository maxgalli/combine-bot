REPLY 2 (by pkeicher @ 2024-10-09T08:13:35.728Z):
Hi @santocch ,
sorry for the late reply! I noticed that you don’t use the --toysFreq when generating the toys, i.e. you are not using frequentist toys for your test. Using this option is recommended (see the docs) as you then sample the toys from the posterior likelihood which should be much more similar to the data. This might expect why the value you obtain for your data_obs object is different. Is there a specific reason you want to perform this test with a-priori toys? If not, can you try it again with the --toysFreq option?
Cheers,
Philip
