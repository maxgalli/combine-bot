REPLY 2 (by kcormier @ 2023-06-28T13:35:18.639Z):
Hi Sima,
If I’ve understood correctly that you are trying to get a breakdown of the uncertainties into different sources for your fit, you should take a look at this section of the documentation:
https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part3/nonstandard/#breakdown-of-uncertainties
As you can see, running such a breakdown requires running a number of additional fits, and defining an ordering in which the uncertainties are applied. So this is not done by the default fits.
Also, just to clarify, the syst branch of the limit tree output by combine is indicating whether or not the fit was run with systematics, its not indicating the size of the statistical uncertainties.
I hope this answers your questions, if not please feel free to follow up.
