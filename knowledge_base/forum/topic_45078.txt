REPLY 2 [ACCEPTED ANSWER] (by adewit @ 2024-08-05T08:35:45.085Z):
Dear Hyeon-Seo,
The options that are passed with --X-rtd are sometimes options that are not generally recommended to be used, which is why you don’t find them all documented in the documentation.
First, FITTER_NEWER_GIVE_UP does not exist (FITTER_NEVER_GIVE_UP does), so this command simply does nothing. I note that unless I misread the code, both FITTER_BOUND and FITTER_NEVER_GIVE_UP seem to only do something in case another flag is set, which is not the case in your command.
So in conclusion it looks to me like these options could be removed and will not change anything about the way your fit is run and the outcome.
Best,
Adinda
