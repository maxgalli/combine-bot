REPLY 2 (by agilbert @ 2022-01-24T17:49:39.149Z):
Hi Peter,
This is usually nothing to worry about - this is a log from the minimizer, Minuit. It just means that the parameters have an associated range that the values cannot go beyond during the fit. Usually we set these to be fairly wide by default, so its unlikely to cause a problem. In fact, combine will print an extra warning if it detects a parameter near a boundary.
Cheers,
Andrew
