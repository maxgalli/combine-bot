REPLY 2 (by adewit @ 2022-03-10T17:27:20.492Z):
Dear Prafulla,
One-sided impacts do not necessarily have to be a problem, but you should try to understand if it is expected from your model or not. For example, if CMS_hgg_nuisance_FNUFEB_13TeVscaleCorr has the effect of decreasing the signal normalization, whether you move this parameter up or down by 1 standard deviation, then you would expect to see a one-sided impact like the one you see here.
