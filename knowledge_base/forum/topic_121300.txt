REPLY 4 (by agilbert @ 2025-04-01T09:40:20.816Z):
Hi Andrea,
With --toysFrequentist the POIs are frozen to whichever values they happen to have in the workspace at that moment (*). It’s normally recommended to set these explicitly with --setParameters to be sure the values are the desired ones.
To generate frequentist toys from the global best-fit, the easiest way I can think to do it is to first run a fit with MultiDimFit, save the workspace and a snapshot, then run again, load the snapshot, add --bypassFrequentistFit and the other desired toy generation options.
cheers,
Andrew
