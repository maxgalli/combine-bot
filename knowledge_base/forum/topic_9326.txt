REPLY 4 (by agilbert @ 2022-04-22T10:44:32.526Z):
Leaving the impacts aside, are you even sure the nominal fit is working? If I run:
combine -M MultiDimFit --cminDefaultMinimizerStrategy 0 --rMin -10 --rMax 10 -v 3 --freezeParameters MH -m 125 -d Datacard_Tprime600_Syst.root -n .Test

or
combine -M MultiDimFit --cminDefaultMinimizerStrategy 0 --rMin -10 --rMax 10 -v 3 --freezeParameters MH,pdfindex_THQHadronicTag_13TeV,pdfindex_THQLeptonicTag_13TeV -m 125 -d Datacard_Tprime600_Syst.root -n .Test

both fail, due the PDF going negative in at least one bin. This can be prevented by restricting r to positive values, which may be acceptable if you only intend to set upper lmits, but if you want to give the best-fit value will mean either fixing the issue of the pdf going negative, or using an alternative to the profile likelihood method to determine the confidence intervals.
cheers,
Andrew
