QUESTION (by attikis @ 2026-04-23T09:47:31.574Z):
Dear experts,
I’m trying to understand whether it’s possible to scale nuisance parameters of type “shape” directly from the datacard or the command line (e.g when running text2workspace.py) in the Combine framework.
Is there a support for this action, i.e. to increase up/down variations of a shape systematics without adding the actual histograms inside the ROOT files provided to combine.
Best,
Alexandros
----------------------------------------

REPLY 2 (by gortona @ 2026-04-23T12:28:38.236Z):
Dear Alexandros,
I hope I understood your question correctly. You would like to use the same Up/Down histograms, but associating them to a stronger or weaker effect than the standard 1-sigma one, is it right?
In this case you can just assign a number different from 1 in the datacard. Combine will scale the unit gaussian used for the interpolation by that number (https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/latest/part2/settinguptheanalysis/#template-shape-uncertainties)
Cheers,
Giacomo
