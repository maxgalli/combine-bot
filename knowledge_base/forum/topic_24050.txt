I wanted to ask, dear experts, if Combine has issues dealing with large yield histograms, and, in general, how the issues I find can be overcome.

REPLY 6 (by vischia @ 2023-05-19T13:04:52.000Z):
Hi Giacomo,
if you have a uniform very fine binning you could have problems in the least populated bins because of the limited number of events.
I would suggest a non-uniform binning where the lower-stat bins (deciding in advance some threshold) are kept merged together (in practice, this results in finer binning in the bulk of the distribution, and coarser in the tails).
Cheers,
Pietro
