REPLY 2 (by ntrevisa @ 2024-11-09T19:40:22.358Z):
Hi all,
I am one of the conveners, and what we are requesting is a data/MC comparison where the total uncertainty band is shown for the MC prediction.
The approach we typically use is to sum in quadrature all the uncertainties (lnN and shape ones), with the idea of having a meaningful estimate of the prediction agreement with data. This is valid since the nuisances are independent, and allows to produce the plots without using Combine or performing any fit.
I hope this helps in understanding our request.
Best,
Nicolò

REPLY 3 (by anigamov @ 2024-11-11T15:35:02.978Z):
Hi Jieun,
Just wanted to add that you can produce pre-fit and post-fit plots with FitDiagnostics as described here, alternatively you can do it with PostFitShapesFromWorkspace from CombineHarvester with the --prefit option. Anyway for the pre-fit plots both of these methods will sum the uncertainties in quadrature, which is exactly what you want to do, and one can do it relatively easily starting from the datacards.
