REPLY 2 (by adewit @ 2022-03-21T20:07:02.764Z):
Dear Kuldeep,
From a quick look at your analysis review hypernews I assume that the question is about treating the statistical uncertainties in the MC templates correctly in the fit, is that right?
You can include these by adding
* autoMCStats 0 at the end of your datacard (so below the es line). When the workspace is created, nuisance parameters for each bin will be added, based on the TH1 bin errors in your input histograms. The names of the parameters the form prop_[channelname]_bin[binnumber] and, once they are in the workspace, if you calculate the impacts on that workspace these parameters will also appear in the impact plot
You can find more detail about the addition of MC statistical uncertainties in the Combine documentation [*].
Hope this helps,
Adinda
[*] https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part2/bin-wise-stats/
