Dear Pallabi,
I don’t think you should worry about it, but just to be on the safe side it could be a good idea to try to run combine MultiDimFit once freezing and once not freezing the CMS*_NPS25003_EMB_scale_t_DM1_*2017 parameter. I expect the two result to be very close.
