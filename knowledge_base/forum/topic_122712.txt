REPLY 2 (by adewit @ 2025-04-08T14:33:10.784Z):
Dear Ganesh,
yes, it’s expected - this tells combine to look for the shape templates if they exist, and otherwise treat the uncertainty as lognormal. It is mentioned in the documentation, with a little bit more information, here: https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/latest/part2/settinguptheanalysis/#template-shape-uncertainties (you have to scroll down to the “warning” box)
Best,
Adinda
