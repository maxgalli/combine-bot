REPLY 4 (by anigamov @ 2024-08-27T09:59:09.063Z):
Dear Ming-Yan,
Alright, I think whether you can assume that k_c does not affect other Higgs production processes should be discussed in the HIG PAG.
On the technical side, for the case when you also want to float bkg-H component in the fit together with k_c I would just assume that bkg-H is a background process (by setting process identifier > 0 as discussed here), and use a rateParam to scale the bkg-H component. Then you can use the same QuadraticScaling signal model as in the 1POI case.
Best,
Aliya
