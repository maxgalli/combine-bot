REPLY 2 (by adewit @ 2023-10-18T09:04:44.245Z):
Hi Sasha,
I think this message was supposed to go to the statistical interpretation tools forum (subforum of Common Analysis Tools): https://cms-talk.web.cern.ch/c/physics/cat/cat-stats/279 - in future it would be better to follow up there.
First: presumably you are not still using CMSSW_7_1_5? The latest recommended Combine version runs in CMSSW_11.
The option --setPhysicsModelParameters was renamed to ‘setParameters’ several years ago. The common command line options are detailed here: https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part3/runningthetool/#common-command-line-options
By the way, a lot of tutorial material was produced over the last years, it might be helpful to have a look at some of it: https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part4/usefullinks/ ( and https://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part5/longexercise/, one of the sets of exercises used in the tutorials).
Best,
Adinda
