QUESTION (by hnguyen @ 2023-04-18T17:22:54.534Z):
Dear all,
I am trying to build a fit model including a signal region and a control region of QCD multijet background. In this model the QCD background in the signal region correlates with QCD in the control region using a transfer function so that QCD background in the signal region can be estimated from data in the control region.
I followed the example here:
http://cms-analysis.github.io/HiggsAnalysis-CombinedLimit/part3/nonstandard/ (RooParametricHist gammaN for shapes)
At first, in other to follow as close as possible this example, I subtracted all other backgrounds (top, V+jets …) from data and fit the fit model of signal and only QCD background to this non-QCD background subtracted data. The fit converges using MultiDimFit method.
However, when I do not subtract non-QCD backgrounds from data but include them in the fit, the fit produces unreasonable values (~0) with some messages as seen in [1]. Since the above example does not show explicitly how to include other backgrounds in the fit model, I am not sure what I am doing is correct. Can you please take a look at the data card to see if this is the right way to include other backgrounds in the fit (dataCard_allBkgr.txt)? If the fit works when other backgrounds are subtracted from data, why doesn’t it when they are included in the fit model?
In the fit_QCD.tar you can find two data cards and supported files to perform the fits (combine -M MultiDimFit -v 3):

dataCard_allBkgr.txt: data card includes all backgrounds
dataCard_onlyQCD.txt: data card includes only QCD. Other backgrounds are subtracted from data

Thank you in advance for your help,
Duong

REPLY 2 (by adewit @ 2023-04-19T15:18:27.860Z):
Dear Duong,
Can you try including the other backgrounds in the same way you did it for the signal? Ie, convert the TH1F to a RooDataHist, and see if that helps?
If that doesn’t work, maybe others have better suggestions.
Best,
Adinda
PS By the way, are you including real data in the signal region? If so remember that you should run MultiDimFit on an asimov dataset (-t -1) - you can also use channel masking to fit the control region by itself, if you need to.
