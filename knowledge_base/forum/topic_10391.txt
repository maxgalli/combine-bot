REPLY 4 (by agilbert @ 2022-05-17T09:54:14.829Z):
Hi Minxi,
I ran your example script. As far as I can tell, the binning is consistent, but you need to take care of the overflow (data points with value > 100). These are effectively ignored when you convert from TH1 to RooDataHist, however it appears the constructor from RooDataSet will put them in the last bin. If I exclude these when building the RooDataSet, they appear identical (see attached).
