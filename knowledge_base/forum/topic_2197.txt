From the file name, 'CMS_Hemu_13TeV_multipdf.root', I believe you are looking at the H->emu, which is likely a search for tiny signal over a moderate background.
Usually, in this type of searches the signal systematics will be small or negligible, wrt the statistical component of the signal. For what concern the background, the emu final state should prefer functional forms with a small number of degrees of freedom to be model, potentially being linear. In this regime the only relevant nuisance left is the background normalization.
