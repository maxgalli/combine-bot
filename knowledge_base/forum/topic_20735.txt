REPLY 3 (by gortona @ 2023-02-24T14:23:14.000Z):
Dear Maida,
You can try calling
ulimit -s unlimited
before running text2workspace.
Although I would also encourage you to check if all the shape nuisances you are applying are actually meaningful and couldn’t be replaced by lnN. For example by running the card validation tools or by checking that the up/down shapes are indeed different.
Cheers,
Giacomo
