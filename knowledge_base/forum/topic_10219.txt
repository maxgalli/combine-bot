REPLY 6 (by malcles @ 2022-05-09T15:19:37.000Z):
Hi Riccardo,
You can do a 2D fit directly in fact (as done in HH-> bb gamma gamma analysis for example), you do not need sPlot.
sPlot would rather be doing a 1D fit with invariant mass only to estimate the q2 distribution of signal or background. You are not supposed to use the variable you want to infer with sweights
as a discriminating variable in the fit in sPlots.
Cheers,
Julie
