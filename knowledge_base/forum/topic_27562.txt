REPLY 2 (by anigamov @ 2023-07-26T10:09:18.594Z):
Dear Matej,
The FitDiagnostics method in Combine does a lot of things and the minimisation is often ran in several iterations. In this case I think you are looking at the messages from the initial fit to the nusaincePdf which is done to save the snapshot of pre-fit nuisancePdf which can be used later for the toys. Perhaps someone else wants to give a more detailed explanation.
But I think you are trying to understand why the fit failed. I’d suggest to increase the verbosity by one level i.e. -v 3 so we have more information to debug.
Best,
Aliya
