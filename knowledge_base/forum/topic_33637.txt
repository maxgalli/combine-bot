REPLY 4 (by anigamov @ 2024-01-25T10:15:34.152Z):
Dear @sbein,
As @nmangane mentioned the fit_b object corresponds to the post-fit results for background-only fit.
But both fit_b and fit_s contain the shifts wrt to pre-fit NP values, which are conventionally set to 0 for log-normal nuisance parameters, more details at [1] from Nick’s message above.
The post-fit shapes and normalisation including the rates and shapes modifications from all NP are saved in the fitDiagnosticsTest.root file as described here and also in one of the tutorials here.
Best,
Aliya
