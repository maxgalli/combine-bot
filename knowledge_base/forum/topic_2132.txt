REPLY 2 (by amarini @ 2021-07-28T07:48:53.000Z):
*** Discussion title: Higgs Combination
Hi,
these lines are actually for the regularization terms.
We may indeed need to understand if we have to propagate them in combineCards.
However, for any practical usage, regularization (and its optimization) will be tight to the final datacard configuration, content, and POIs. So very unlikely need to be used as is in combination of cards.
In your case, it is likely that the Hgg and Hzz (high resolution) channels will take this step on their shoulder and likely you won't need it. So you can start to remove it. The low-resolution channel will still contribute to the sum of the bins accordingly to their resolution.

-Andrea
