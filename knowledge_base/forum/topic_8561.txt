QUESTION (by xuyan @ 2022-03-23T22:00:36.541Z):
Hello experts,
I am writing to see if there is a way to access the fitted error band within the combine tool, with combine -M FitDiagnostics? In the bare RooFit, one can fit the data, get the RooFitResult and plot the fitted model with VisualizeError(). However, combine -M FitDiagnostics only gives the fits as TH1F histograms and data as TGraphAsymmErrors. I am not very sure how to add the fitting error band on top of them…
Do you have some suggestions?
Thanks a lot!
Sebastian
----------------------------------------

REPLY 2 (by amarini @ 2022-03-23T23:01:47.000Z):
–saveWithUncertainties
--saveWithUncertainties Save also pre/post-fit uncertainties on
the shapes and normalizations (from
resampling the covariance matrix)

-Andrea
