REPLY 2 (by gortona @ 2026-01-29T13:44:37.928Z):
Dear Steffi,
It is a bit strange to get the error you quote in the case of very large r. I would expect it for large negative values r, but not for positive values. In any case, I don’t think you should trust the limits you get after that error.
Can you please send us more details on your setup (signal and background yields, the range of the signal strength you are testing, the command you are using  and any other relevant detail) and/or the datacard you are using?
