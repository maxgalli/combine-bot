REPLY 6 (by nckw @ 2021-08-16T09:50:09.000Z):
Hi Maxi,
Ok, at least it is encouraging to see that the likelihoods are not too dissimilar. Can you extend the range? In order to get an idea about how the limits will agree, we need to see where in mu is the the crossing between the likelihood value at mu=0 and ~3.
With the limit calculation, there is the complication of calculating the “p_b” part and I am not sure the code provided in the Twiki (the RooFit based one) is validated for cases where the signal strength best fit is very negative.
So in short, I would extend the range to see at least approximately where a limit would be placed and if the likelihood scans agree well in the region crossing 2*deltaNLL = 2-4, then I think the closure is ok.
Best,
Nick
