REPLY 2 (by anigamov @ 2023-06-07T17:01:38.437Z):
Dear @amkaur,
Sorry for a delayed response. Let me first try to understand what are you trying to do.
You mentioned unbinned fit but at the same time you consider bkg templates (binned) in your model, it can not be both, so help me to understand please.
A technical point for the datacard, I think you should define data_obs in the same format as signal and background processes (RooDataHist). Then when you run the text2workspace.py you can scale di-muon and di-electron processes with common POI and extract the ratio, i.e.
text2workspace.py temp_datacard.txt  -m 125 -o test_1.root  -P HiggsAnalysis.CombinedLimit.PhysicsModel:multiSignalModel  --PO verbose  --PO 'map=control/el_DY_S:r_el[1,0,10]'  --PO 'map=signal/mu_DY_S:r_mu=expr;;r_mu("@0*@1",r_el,R[1,0,2])'

where R = r_mu/r_el.
