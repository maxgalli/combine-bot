REPLY 2 (by anigamov @ 2024-07-24T15:47:23.762Z):
Dear Hyeon-Seo Yun,
The fit with the floating pdf index fails because you set --cminDefaultMinimizerStrategy=1 , which triggers Hessian calls, and it just does not work with discrete parameters. That is why it is recommended to use --cminDefaultMinimizerStrategy=0 for models with discrete profiling as described here.
Best,
Aliya
