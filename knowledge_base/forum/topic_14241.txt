QUESTION (by mmasciov @ 2022-08-23T19:19:21.765Z):
Dear experts,
We are reaching out as we are having a few issues we are unable to understand and thus solve, and we hope you may help.
We are performing an unbinned shape analysis where signal is modeled with a double Crystal Ball plus a Gaussian PDF, while background is modeled using a RooMultiPDF consisting of exponential, power-law and Bernstein polynomial functions (discrete profiling).
This is done in two different channels (“ch1” and “ch2”), as well for their combination.
In addition, we also define a third channel (“ch0”) that equals the sum of “ch1” and “ch2”.
We believe we were able to correctly build our datacards.
We copied example datacards obtained using a toy MC RooDataSet, together with the corresponding workspaces, at:
/afs/cern.ch/user/m/mmasciov/public/cards_forTest/
No warning is printed when the datacards are validated using combineTool, and we can’t identify anything suspicious when inspecting the corresponding workspaces either.
However, when we attempt to use any combine method (AsymptoticLimits, FitDiagnostics, MultiDimFit, etc.) the job is killed for no apparent reason (even when verbosity is increased no information about the reason why the jobs has been killed is printed).
We have also tried different combine options as suggested in the documentation (among others: --cminDefaultMinimizerStrategy 0, and/or --X-rtd MINIMIZER_freezeDisassociatedParams), with no success.
On the other hand, when using/importing in the workspace individual background PDFs rather than a RooMultiPDF object consisting of multiple PDFs, at least a subset of the methods appear to succeed.
Still, it is unclear to us what is wrong (if any) with our model and/or with our datacards, and we are unable to understand what the underlying issue may be .
Any suggestion would be welcome and much appreciated!
Many thanks in advance,
Mario (for the involved analysis team)

REPLY 6 (by amarini @ 2022-08-25T09:32:50.000Z):
Hi Mario,
you should check that your model (signal) makes sense.
You are floating alphaL,alphaR,mcfracL,mcfracR,nL,nR,sigma,
that should be fixed (or constrained to some value).
Also, you should check that all individual background pdfs converges. For instance, ch1 index 3.
For ch2, you should probably think to use lower order poly, because with 3 or 4 events it will be difficult to constrain the background.
Cheers,
-Andrea
