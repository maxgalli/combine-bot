REPLY 2 (by anigamov @ 2024-05-13T10:13:53.083Z):
Hi Mintu,
I would look into the RooFit results more because the estimated distance to minimum value seems too large (10^6) to consider the fit results from RooFit satisfactory (in Combine it is required to be < 1e-4).
I think you are just comparing different points in the minimization process.
In general Combine minimization routine uses sequential algorithm and is more complex than fitTo method, you can find more information here.
