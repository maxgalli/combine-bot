REPLY 3 (by amarini @ 2024-07-04T10:56:30.651Z):
iirc in the rooberbstein there is one degenerate dof, which can be reassorbed in the total normalization. You may need to freeze the first coeff.
In the RooBernsteinFast, this does not happen. Here the first coeff should always be 1.
Note that the RooBernsteinFast were originally developed to have analytical integrals and are in combine.
